Getting started

Up in three steps.

No SDK. No code. No developer required.

01

Add @CanvasVerificationBot to your group

Search for @CanvasVerificationBot in Telegram and add it to your group. This takes about thirty seconds and doesn't require any technical configuration at this stage.

02

Set it as admin

Go to your group settings and promote @CanvasVerificationBot to admin. It needs permission to mute members and send messages. Those are the only two permissions required. Nothing else needs to change in your group.

03

Configure your settings

DM the bot directly to set your payout wallet, verification cadence, topic category, and mod revenue splits. Or open the group owner dashboard if you prefer a visual interface. Once configured, Canvas matches your group to active advertiser campaigns and you start earning from the very next join.
