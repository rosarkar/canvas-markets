How it works

Three steps.
Then it runs itself.

No ongoing work. No dashboards to check. No invoices to send.

01

Add @CanvasVerificationBot to your group

Search for the bot in Telegram and add it to your group.

02

Set it as admin

The bot needs admin permissions to intercept join requests and send private DMs. That's the only permission required.

03

Configure your settings

DM the bot or use the group owner dashboard to set your verification frequency, payout splits for mods, and campaign preferences. Then you're live.

For group owners

Your members are your inventory.

You built the community. Advertisers want access to it. Canvas connects them and pays you per verified join. No brand deals, no pitch emails, no minimum size requirement.

  • Starts at $0.05 per join. High-intent groups earn more. The market sets the price.
  • Works for any Telegram group, any topic, any size
  • Set how often members re-verify: every join, every 30 days, or any cadence you choose
  • Revenue splits for mods and admins, paid automatically to each wallet

For advertisers

Pay for verified humans, not impressions.

Your task reaches people who are already in the community you care about. After verification, the bot can connect them directly to your agent, turning every join into a warm lead, not just a data point.

  • Target by group topic, size, and member profile
